# Sage

Sage is an executive productivity assistant built for the GenAI Academy hackathon.

The product is designed to help a leader or operator move through a practical workflow:
- review today's priorities
- prepare for important meetings
- capture and debrief meeting notes
- turn follow-through into action items
- manage weekly load and decision risk

## Hackathon Context

This repository was prepared for a GenAI Academy hackathon submission focused on multi-agent productivity workflows, meeting intelligence, and action-oriented executive assistance.

## Product Areas

- `Today`: executive command center
- `Week Planner`: weekly planning and workflow routing
- `Meeting Brief`: meeting preparation with context and decisions
- `Debrief`: post-meeting extraction of actions and follow-through
- `Tasks`: prioritization and execution queue
- `Insights`: executive operating signals
